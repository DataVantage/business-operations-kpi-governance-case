## Key Findings

- Gross Revenue: approx. 10.64m €
- Net Revenue: approx. 9.75m €
- Adjustment Revenue: approx. -896.81k €
- Cancellation Rate: approx. 14.81%
- Known Customer Revenue: approx. 8.30m €
- Orders without CustomerID Rate: approx. 14.32%
